Goals in Flux: Abusing Genetic Algorithms for Aesthetic Gains

An art project that derives images from genetic algorithms. Individuals are represented as triangles and populations are represented as columns of triangles. The image can be read from left to right, where populations evolve over successive iterations.

Source: github.com/0la0/gaTriangles

Figure 6ae0f29.

The algorithm that drives the procedure follows:

Let ub be an upper bound for a fitness function.
Let lb a lower bound for a fitness function.
For a predetermined number of iterations, do:

  • Step 1: Generate an arbitrary goal state s and an arbitrary gene index i
  • Step 2: While genei is less than ub, iterate the algorithm.
  • Step 3: While genei is greater than lb, increase the mutation level and iterate the algorithm.
  • Step 4: Go to step 1.
